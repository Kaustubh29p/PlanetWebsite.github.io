FAQ's About Space

Q1-Why is pluto not a planet?

Answer-Pluto is small, so it is not a planet, it is a dwarf planet.

Q2-What is the brightest planet in the night sky?

Answer-Venus is the brightest.

Q3- Which planet has the most moons?

Answer-Saturn

Q4-Which planet has the least moons?

Answer-Mercury and Venus.

Q5-Which Planet is known as the morning star?

Answer-Venus

Q6-Which planet is known as the evening star?

Answer-Venus again

Q7-Which planet has the most volcanos?

Answer-Mars has the most volcanoes.
